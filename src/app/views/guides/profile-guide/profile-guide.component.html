My Profile

When you register on {{g.title}}, you get a profile where you can manage your information and reset your password.

On your profile, you can manage your information and reset your password.

To access your profile, click your name in the navigation bar at the top of the site and click on "Profile".

If you are verified, your profile also displays verification details. Contact a verifier or moderator if you want to learn more.

Setting Player / Island Name Visibility

By default, your player and island names are abbreviated, so e.g. "Tooie" from "Willowby" will be abbreviated to "T" from "W" for other players. (moderators and admins can always see your full names)

To change this and publically display your player or island name, go to your profile and click on the . When the becomes a green , your name will be visible to other players.

Clicking on the again hides the player or island name from other players again and the name will instead be abbreviated like before.
